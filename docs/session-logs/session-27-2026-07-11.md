## Notes

- **A component that stays mounted and renders `null` (rather than being
  conditionally rendered by its parent) doesn't get its `useState`
  initializer re-run between logical "instances."** `NamePromptModal`'s
  reset bug and the general shape of `ConfirmModal`/`NamePromptModal`'s
  pattern are the same across this codebase — worth remembering for any
  future modal built the same way.
- **`activeFrame`, like `activeLayerId`/`activeGridId`, is deliberately
  excluded from this app's undo snapshots** (`contentSnapshot` in
  `store.js`) — a working-session pointer, not artwork content. Any new
  action that mutates one of these pointers as a side effect (like
  `reorderFrame`'s "keep the moved frame selected" behavior) needs to
  account for that pointer not being reverted by undo, even though the
  content it points at is.
- **Hook-count-mismatch errors during live manual testing can be an HMR
  artifact, not a real bug** — specifically when hooks were just removed
  from the middle of a component via a live file edit. A full page reload
  is the fast way to tell the two apart, same lesson as session 26's
  stale-console-history and flaky-screenshot notes (Browser-pane/dev-loop
  quirks worth checking before assuming the app itself is broken).
- Documentation cleanup went through one real mid-review correction: the
  first proposed plan would have deleted `docs/tool-roadmap.md` along with
  the other three stale docs, but the user wanted to keep it since they're
  still actively using/merging it — a good example of why plan mode's
  review step matters even for "obviously mechanical" cleanup work, since
  what looks uniformly stale from the outside isn't always uniformly done
  from the user's side.
